A running list of things I've built or am tinkering with. Some are practical,
some are just me scratching an itch. Most start with "how hard could it be?"

### 🩺 Preeklamsia Early Recognition Tool (PERT)

A lightweight, offline-friendly screening aid for early recognition of
preeclampsia — runs entirely in the browser, stores entries locally, and exports
to CSV. Built for real-world clinical note-taking on a phone.

→ [Open the tool](/tools/preeklamsia-early-recognition-tool/)
